Overview

TAKAH is a score counter app. It does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or email.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store personal information directly. If the app includes third-party advertising (such as Google AdMob), those services may collect non-personal data automatically (e.g., device type, operating system, advertising identifiers, and general usage data) to display and measure ads.

Ads (Google AdMob)

TAKAH may display banner ads using Google AdMob. Google may collect non-personal information to serve and measure ads in accordance with Google's policies. You may be able to opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (where available).

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Non-personal data may be shared with advertising providers only as necessary to operate ads and improve performance.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

TAKAH is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
